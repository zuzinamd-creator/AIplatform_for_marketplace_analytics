# Release Notes — v0.6-mvp-intelligence

**Release date:** 2026-06-07  
**Codename:** MVP Intelligence — Inventory + Priority Calibration  
**Audit decision:** GO ([phase 630b report](../ai/phase_630b_priority_calibration_report.md))

---

## Highlights

This release completes the **Period Intelligence MVP**: deterministic domain analysts, Business Coverage V1, Executive Summary v2, **Inventory Intelligence**, and **priority calibration** that keeps revenue/profit at the center of seller-facing AI.

---

## Phase 6.2.1 — Deterministic Analysts & Quality Audit

- 10 domain analysts wired through governed signals
- Recommendation audit: Insight / Dashboard Echo / False Positive / Data Quality
- Deep period insights (unprofitable SKUs, high logistics/commission)
- GO/NO-GO quality gates in audit scripts

## Phase 6.2.2 — Business Coverage & Executive Summary v2

- Business Coverage V1 (50% with pilot data)
- Executive summary v2 persisted in recommendations
- Root cause confidence, analysis limitations, advertising warning
- `lineage.insight_engine_version: coverage_v1`
- Migration audit + backfill scripts

## Phase 6.3 Architecture Blueprint

- Full data-domain audit (39 PostgreSQL tables)
- Roadmap for Ads, Tax, OPEX, Conversion expansion
- Operating Director scaffold design (not production)

## Phase 6.3.0 — Inventory Intelligence Activation

- **New:** `app/domain/inventory/intelligence.py` — dead stock, slow movers, frozen capital, concentration, risk
- **Enhanced:** `InventoryAnalyst` — 5 insight types with Russian statements
- Inventory wired into governed signals, executive aggregator, deep insights
- **Results:** Inventory Insight Rate 0% → 100%, Sub-Coverage 25% → 75%
- **Side effect:** Inventory L1 over-prioritized → Seller Usefulness dropped to 68.2

## Phase 6.3.0B — Priority Calibration Fix

- **Revenue Protection Layer** — primary from revenue/profit unless escalated inventory IQ > revenue + 8 pp
- **Inventory Escalation Rules** — L1 only for critical dead stock / high frozen capital
- **Executive rebalancing** — domain-balanced lead slots, max 1 inventory block
- **Inventory deduplication** — semantic buckets, no duplicate lead meaning
- **Results:** Seller Usefulness **80.3**, AI Readiness **86.1**, Inventory Insight Rate preserved at 100%

## Phase 6.4.0–6.4.2 — Documentation

- README modernization and cutover
- Documentation hub, archive, and release structure

## Upgrade notes

1. Run `alembic upgrade head` (no new migrations required for 6.3.0B logic-only change)

```bash
.venv/bin/python scripts/backfill_ai_recommendations.py --user-id <UUID> --all-reports
```

```bash
.venv/bin/python scripts/phase_630_inventory_audit.py --limit 4
```
