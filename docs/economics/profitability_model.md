# Profitability model (seller economics)

Profitability is computed from governed projections; payout is tracked but **never treated as profit**.

## Definitions

- Revenue: sum of `sale`
- Returns: abs(sum of `return`)
- Fees: commissions/logistics/storage/ads/penalties/acquiring/deductions (absolute sums)
- COGS: effective-dated unit cost × units sold

## Contribution margin

\[
CM = (Revenue - Returns + Compensation) - Fees - COGS
\]

Margin %:

\[
Margin\% = \frac{CM}{Revenue} \times 100
\]

## Why payout != profit

Payout is settlement cashflow; it can include timing and reconciliation differences and must not be part of profitability math.
